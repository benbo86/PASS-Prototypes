What's still open

  • Manual close/reopen vs. one continuous thread — the built model lets office close any 1:1 at will; Simon, Charlotte, and Here to Care all independently downplay it for 1:1s (for three different reasons). Not yet decided whether to change the default or make it configurable.
  • Inbox scoping by area — Simon doesn't want to see messages from carers outside his region. Area tags already exist on employees and customers, which looks like the natural mechanism, but this is a theory, not a design.
  • Auto-close on employment end — now asked for independently three times (Ben, Simon, Charlotte), but knowingly parked for now rather than built.
  • Messaging a customer's family member directly — new idea from Here to Care, explicitly ruled out of scope for v1. Revisit post-launch if it resurfaces.
